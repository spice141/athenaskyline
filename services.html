Services

Aerial Surveying

Putting the super high-resolution drone cameras to task and utilizing their precise GPS abilities, our drones can accurately survey terrain down to millimeter accuracy. This data can be used by surveyors for ground works cut and fill, or pile volumetric measurements. Working closely with your surveyors, we can survey vast areas of terrain with fast and incredibly accurate results down to 10mm. Unlike traditional surveying, we can deliver hundreds of thousands of millimeter-accurate measurement points across a site within 72 hours. Our aerial surveys reduce human risk on busy construction sites and ensure that there is minimal interruption to ground works.

Aerial Inspections

Utilizing the super high-resolution camera and precise hovering abilities, drones can carefully inspect structures and objects for defects, avoiding any unnecessary requirements to put inspection crews in danger or working at heights. The drones we fly are equipped with precision obstacle avoidance. This means drones can be flown closer than ever to structures without risk of collision. The high-resolution drone cameras can inspect at millimeter level accuracy to check for defects. The Drones flown by us uses the autonomous systems that pick up from where they left off and images hold all the key metadata including precise GPS location and altitude.

Aerial Mapping

Mapping Data from Drones produce Millimeter level accurate maps using high resolution imagery taken by Drones and processed into large scale, pinpoint accurate topographical maps. The drones intelligently calculate flight paths for any given area, the level of detail required, and amount of flying required. In fully autonomous mode, the drones can be trusted to take off and land themselves on larger projects, immediately carrying on with the task once the battery has been replaced. Our Drones can fly the maximum time over a single battery even up to 45-50min and covers squares of kilometers of mapped land. With a huge surplus of batteries as well as on site rapid recharging facilities, we can survey huge areas of land in a single day.

Asset Surveillance & Monitoring

We use advanced Drone Platforms for performing stealthy and silent surveillance missions, with the ability to preplan the flight missions and to automatically capture the required imagery (including the necessary overlap and height requirements). We offer aerial data capture including night vision, thermal imaging and long-range high- definition zoom cameras. We also offer Ground Based Drone based Services that allow a drone to hover for 24 hours a day without the need to land. Our Drone Fleet are fully autonomous with advanced waypoint mission capabilities that can be activated 24 hours a day with or without human involvement.
